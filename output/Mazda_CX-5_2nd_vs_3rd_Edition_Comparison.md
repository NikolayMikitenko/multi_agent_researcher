# Key Differences Between Mazda CX-5 2nd and 3rd Editions

## Executive Summary
The Mazda CX-5 3rd generation (2026 model) introduces significant updates over the 2nd generation (2017-2025 models) in design, features, technology, and performance. The new model is larger, more spacious, and incorporates advanced technology and refined interior materials, while initially retaining similar engine options with hybrid powertrains planned for the near future. This report highlights the key differences to help potential buyers and enthusiasts understand the evolution of the CX-5.

## Key Findings

### Design
- The 3rd generation CX-5 is 4.5 inches longer in overall length and wheelbase, and over half an inch wider, resulting in a more spacious cabin and cargo area.
- Features a bolder, more chiseled front end with slimmer LED headlights and a wider grille featuring Mazda's Signature Wing design.
- Rear design inspired by larger Mazda SUVs (CX-70, CX-90) with slimmer, darker taillights and "MAZDA" lettering replacing the traditional badge.
- Glossy black trim on grille, wheel arches, and side skirts adds a rugged yet refined look.
- Continues Mazda's Kodo design language with a new interpretation emphasizing stability and capability.

### Features
- More spacious interior with increased rear legroom, knee, and headroom.
- Larger door openings for easier ingress and egress.
- Available panoramic sunroof enhances cabin openness.
- Ambient lighting with seven color options adds to comfort.
- Larger and easier-to-load cargo area with a lower lift-in height.
- Materials and finishes emphasize Japanese craftsmanship and a refined atmosphere.

### Technology
- New available 15.6-inch integrated touchscreen, the largest ever in a Mazda, with Google built-in for apps and AI assistant integration.
- Enhanced advanced driver assistance systems (ADAS).
- Improved infotainment system with smartphone-like usability and customizable home screen.

### Performance
- The 3rd generation retains the current 2.5L inline-4 engine at launch, with no new engine initially.
- A new hybrid powertrain is planned for 2027.
- The 2nd generation offered 2.5L naturally aspirated and turbocharged engines, AWD only from 2022 refresh, and a short-lived diesel option.

## Analysis
The 3rd generation Mazda CX-5 represents a significant evolution in size, design, and technology compared to the 2nd generation. While the performance remains largely unchanged at launch, the introduction of hybrid powertrains will modernize the lineup. The interior improvements and technology upgrades position the CX-5 competitively in the compact SUV segment, appealing to buyers seeking a premium and spacious vehicle with advanced features.
